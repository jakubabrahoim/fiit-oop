# Course Information

## Assignment Topic

**Game** - students choose a genre that must be unique within their seminar group.

## Requirements for Completion

To successfully complete the course, you need to:

- Earn at least **30 points** during the semester
- Earn at least **4 points** from the test
- Submit all parts of the assignment
