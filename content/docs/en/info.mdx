# Course Information

## Assignment Topic

**Game** - students choose a genre, which must not be the same as that chosen by their classmates. The assignment as a whole will be divided into three parts or submissions: specification, checkpoint, and final submission.

## Requirements for Completion

To successfully obtain credit (be able to attent the final exam), it is necessary to:

- Earn at least **30 points** during the semester
- Earn at least **4 points** from the test
- Submit all parts of the assignment
