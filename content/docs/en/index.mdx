# Object-Oriented Programming

Welcome to the official page of the **Object-Oriented Programming** course at the Faculty of Informatics and Information Technologies, STU in Bratislava.

## About the Course

This course focuses on:

- **Object-Oriented Programming** - principles and paradigms
- **Java** - from basics to advanced concepts
- **Software Development Fundamentals** - Design, Git, implementation, testing
