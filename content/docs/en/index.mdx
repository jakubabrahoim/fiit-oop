## About the Course

This course has three goals:

1. **New paradigm of thinking** – to teach you a new way of thinking about programming. We will start from the basics and gradually move to more complex concepts that you will also practice.
2. **OOP in practice (Java)** – to give you space through a project and exercises to master object-oriented programming in Java.
3. **Software development principles** – to build good habits in you from the beginning. We want you to understand the importance of design, testing, versioning, and software development as such.
